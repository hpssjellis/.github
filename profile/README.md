# WebMCU-AI Research Lab
> **Bridging the gap between High School Technology Education and Research-Level TinyML.**

Welcome to the **WebMCU-AI Lab**. Led by a veteran Technology & Robotics educator with over 35 years of experience, this organization focuses on demystifying "Black Box" AI through **Transparent TinyML** and **WebAI** integration.

---

## 🔬 Research Focus
We specialize in **On-Device Machine Learning (TinyML)** with a focus on browser-based interaction via **WebSerial** and **TensorFlow.js**. Our goal is to move beyond simple inference and provide templates for **full training on-device**, ensuring data privacy and local execution.

### Core Pillars:
* **Transparent AI:** Moving away from "Black Box" models toward explainable, logic-driven neural architectures.
* **Inquiry-Path Pedagogy:** A "Craft-Minutes" philosophy that prioritizes foundational numeracy and logic before tool-based abstraction.
* **Edge-to-Web Integration:** Seamless communication between microcontrollers (ESP32-S3, Arduino Portenta H7) and browser interfaces.

## 📋 Coding Standards
To maintain clarity for both researchers and students, all lab repositories follow these standards:
- **Vanilla Everything:** Minimize dependencies; prioritize Single-File HTML and Vanilla JS.
- **Naming Convention:** Descriptive `camelCase` with a `my` prefix for internal functions (e.g., `async function myTrainModel()`).
- **Logic First:** High use of `async/await` over `.then()` promises for readable, linear logic.
